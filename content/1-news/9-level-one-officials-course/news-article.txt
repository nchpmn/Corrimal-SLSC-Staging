Title: Level One Officials Course

----

Text: 

Surf Life Saving Illawarra will be conducting a Level One Officials Course on 11th and 12th October 2014 commencing at 8.00 am at the Surf House.

Anyone who is interested in gaining this qualification please respond directly to corrimalsurfclub@gmail.com (details are also listed on our Facebook page). The cost of the course is $80.00 per person. Our club will cover this cost but we will need to ensure your commitment to complete the course, the post-course assessment and obviously your ongoing commitment to continue to volunteer as an Official.
